Hi I am Isha

Founder of DreamSVin & Bombay Networking Circle

After graduating, I believed I would either enter the pharmacy space or eventually help run my father's surgical business.

But life pulled me in a different direction.

I entered the world of business development and soon started working closely with JITO, one of the largest business foundations in the Jain community.

Over time, I led initiatives nationally and internationally and began working with founders across industries.

Every month, while consulting hundreds of startups, creators, and business owners, I started noticing a clear pattern.

Everyone was growing, but almost everyone was dependent.

Creators were surviving on brand deals.

Coaches were chasing followers or burning money on ads.

Founders were stuck between content, ads, and influencer marketing, with no real stability.

That's when I realised the real problem wasn't growth, it was dependency.

And that realisation led to the creation of the Triple C framework, built on Content, Collaboration, and Community, to help people grow without depending on just one source.

Today, I'm on a mission to teach real networking, not the noisy, transactional kind, but the kind that builds trust, ownership, and long-term results.
